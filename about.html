ABOUT US:

Ruths Letters was founded in December 2023 by Arianna Bains in Sungai Buloh, inspired by the comfort of home-baked cookies and the warmth of handwritten notes. What began as a small batch kitchen soon became a local favorite, sharing heartfelt treats that feel like sweet messages from a loved one, one cookie at a time.

Warm Heart, One Cookie: At Ruths Letters, we believe small things—a cookie, a note—can make a big difference. That’s why every treat is made with love, care, and a personal touch. More than cookies, we create little reminders that someone is thinking of you—like a hug you can taste.

Love, Connection, Simplicity, Joy. These are the 4 principles that Ruth Letters strive to achieve

Eat our cookies.

OUR VISION:

To sprinkle the world with joy. One cookie, one story, one smile at a time.

OUR MOTTO:

We’re here to bake love into every bite. At Ruths Letters, every cookie is a little note of sweetness, handwritten with care, wrapped in warmth, and sent from our kitchen to hearts everywhere.
